Dialog 01

Doctor: Good morning Mr. Jones. What is wrong?

Mr Jones Good morning, Doctor, Williams. I feel very ill. / I am coughing and sneezing. I feel hot and cold all the time. / Whenever I eat, I feel nauseous, like I am going to vomit.

Doctor: Let me take your temperature. / How long have you had the symptoms?

Mr Jones My symptoms started about a day ago.

Doctor: Yes, your temperature is very high. I think you have the flu. / You will have to go home and stay in bed until you feel better. / Drink lots of water so you will not become dehydrated.

Mr Jones Can you give me some medicine?

Doctor: I can give you something to reduce your fever / but you just need to get bed rest. / I will write you a prescription. What pharmacy do you use?

Mr Jones I use Safeway pharmacy, on the corner of Hamilton and Mission. Thank you, doctor.
