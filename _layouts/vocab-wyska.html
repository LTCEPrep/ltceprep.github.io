Vocabulary: WYSAK List

A list of words you should know already

A number of terms found on the vocabulary list included in the LTC Exam study booklet are those that should already be known by a candidate (i.e., test taker).  These terms have been turned into a "Words You Should Know Already" list, or WYSKA (pronounced 'HWIS-kuh') for short.  Almost all of these terms fall in or below the K-4 word list in the English language.  That is, these words are of the four thousand most commonly used words in English, and thereby should already be in a candidate's word bank.  Accordingly, the LTCE Prep course will devote no time studying these terms.  Nonetheless, the list should be reviewed to check if any terms are unfamiliar.
